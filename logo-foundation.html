Dont’s

01

Don't use the white version on any other colour unless the primary and secondary.

02

Don’t recolour the logo in any way, unless the primary colour.

03

Don’t recreate, add effects or change the colours of our logos. Don’t tilt or angle the logo in any way.

04

Don’t stack the logo, or reconfigure it in any way.
